Before you begin

Activating practices use fast breathing and breath holds. They can make you lightheaded.

  • Sit or lie down — never in or near water, or while driving or standing.
  • Stop if you feel dizzy, faint, or short of breath. That feeling is the signal to rest.
  • Skip these if you are pregnant, or have cardiovascular, respiratory, or seizure conditions.
